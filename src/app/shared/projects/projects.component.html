Pay Your Share

Pay Your Share is an innovative web application designed to simplify group payments and streamline the process of sharing expenses with friends and family. With its intuitive interface and seamless integration of the MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express, Angular, Node.js), Pay Your Share offers users a convenient platform to create groups, split payments, and track dues effortlessly. Say goodbye to the hassle of manual calculations and enjoy a hassle-free payment sharing experience with Pay Your Share.

Technology Used:

Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Angular

Backend: Node.Js, Express.Js

Database: MongoDB

Authentication: Google Authentication

Deployment: AWS Amplify
